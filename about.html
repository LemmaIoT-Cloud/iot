About Us.

What We Are!

LemmaIoT Cloud Solution is an Information Technonology agency that offers Enterprise Cloud Solution for SME with Easy and Efficient Cloud Solutions for SME/SMI Companies, Boutique, Hotels and so on.

At LemmaIoT Cloud Solution, We also help individual or corporate to automate their business activities with divers web tools, we also create a platform for hosting your business activities and service.





How We do it

Our services has to do with anything that involves delivering hosted services over the internet, which are broadly divided into three categories

  • Infrastructure-as-a-Service.(IaaS)

    • This is where we integrate your gadgets with internet service to provide a real time service where you will be able to assess and control them anywhere, anytime and whenever you want to.

  • Platform-as-a-Service.(PaaS)

    • PaaS:- This is where we create a platform to solve your define problem, our platform cut across accessibility through any device you are using, i.e. you can access it on you smart form and computers equally.
    For Example; Platform that will automatically create a document from your template at ease.


  • Software-as-a-Service.(SaaS)

    • SaaS:- This is where we develop software application for your personal and business functionalities, the software that will not be beyond your business scope, i.e. an application that can be used on any OS you of your choice. For Example; Android Application, Windows Application, Web-based Application. . .
